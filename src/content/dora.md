# Digital Operational Resilience Act (DORA)
The EU framework for operational resilience in the financial sector.

### Article 19: Incident Reporting
Major ICT-related incidents must be reported to the competent authority within 4 hours.

### Article 24: Threat-Led Penetration Testing
Advanced testing (TLPT) must be performed every 3 years by qualified intelligence-led testers.
