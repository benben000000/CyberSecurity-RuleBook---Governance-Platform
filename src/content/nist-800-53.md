# NIST 800-53 Overview
This control family focuses on access control, auditing, and incident response.

### AC-2: Account Management
Enforce strict least-privilege principles and zero-trust identity checks.

### SI-4: Information System Monitoring
Deploy YARA and Sigma rules to detect anomalies in OT/IT environments.
